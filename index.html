About Us




"Becoming isn’t about arriving somewhere or achieving a certain aim. I see it instead as forward motion, a means of evolving, a way to reach continuously toward a better self. The journey doesn’t end."


Somani Ice-Cream, headquartered in Jamner, Dist-Jalgaon, Maharashtra, has been a prominent player in the Ice-Cream supplier, export and trader industry since its inception in 2024. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Deepak Nitin Somani, the company has established itself as a trusted name in providing high-quality Ice-Cream products to both domestic and international markets. Specializing in Ice-based products, Somani Ice-Cream offers a diverse range of items tailored to meet various consumer needs. Their product portfolio includes A Grade Pista Ice-Cream, Vanila Ice-Cream, Chocolate Ice-Cream, Strawberry Ice-Cream, and Butterscotch Ice-Cream. These Ice-Creams are produced using state-of-the-art processes, ensuring the preservation of the fruit's nutritional value and flavor. In addition to Ice-Cream , the company also exports Cakes and Dairy products, catering to the growing demand for healthy snack options globally. Whether it's the freshness of raw fruits, Somani Ice-Cream maintains rigorous quality standards throughout its production and distribution processes. With a commitment to excellence, Somani Ice-Cream prioritizes customer satisfaction by consistently delivering premium products that exceed expectations. Their adherence to stringent quality control measures, coupled with efficient supply chain management, has earned them a reputation for reliability and integrity in the industry. Beyond business success, Somani Ice-Cream is dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and supporting local Dairy communities. By fostering mutually beneficial relationships with farmers and stakeholders, the company ensures a responsible and ethical supply chain, contributing to the economic development of the region. Driven by a passion for innovation and a dedication to quality, Somani Ice-cream continues to expand its presence in the global market while remaining true to its core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction.
