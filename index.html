About Us




"At Somani Global Exports, we export more than just products; we export trust, quality, and the promise of excellence. From our fields to your hands, always the best."


Somani Global Exports, headquartered in Jamner, Dist-Jalgaon, Maharashtra, India, has been a leading exporter of high-quality banana products since 2024. Under the dynamic leadership of Mrs. Surekha Nitin Somani, we specialize in fresh bananas, banana powder, and banana chips. Sourced directly from Jalgaon, the "Banana City of India," our products are known for their freshness, nutritional value, and superior quality. We utilize advanced techniques to ensure our bananas retain their natural goodness. Our banana powder is a versatile ingredient for health foods and natural sweeteners, while our banana chips, available in various flavors, provide a tasty and healthy snack option. Quality is our top priority; we adhere to stringent quality control measures to meet international standards, ensuring customer satisfaction and building long-term trust. Committed to sustainability, we support local farming communities and promote ethical practices throughout our supply chain. By fostering strong relationships with farmers and stakeholders, we contribute to the region's economic growth while maintaining a responsible business model. Driven by a passion for quality and innovation, Somani Global Exports is expanding its global footprint, staying true to our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction.
